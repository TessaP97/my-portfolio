About us

Vertex HVAC

Vertex So Cal, Inc. was established in 2019 to provide the best HVAC and dehumidification services to the industrial and commercial clients across the state of California. Based in Fontana, CA, ours is a women-owned small business. Tessa Porras, the President leads the company. We have an excellent reputation for our integrity, industry experience and fast service. This has earned us a number of loyal clients, who continue to refer their friends and families. Regardless of the size of the project, we offer the same kind of attention to detail from the beginning to the end.
